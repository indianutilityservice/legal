2. Information We Collect

Account Information:

  • Full name, phone number, and password (stored encrypted)
  • Referral code and sponsor details
  • Account activation date

Vendor-Specific Information:

  • Shop name, business category, and pincode
  • KYC documents: PAN number, Aadhaar number, GST number (if applicable)
  • Bank account details: bank name, account number, IFSC code, UPI ID
  • Payment QR code image (if uploaded)

Transaction Information:

  • Scan & Pay payment records (amount, timestamp, vendor/user IDs)
  • Recharge and BBPS service records
  • Wallet balance and settlement history

We do NOT collect: Location data, contacts, camera access (except QR upload), or any background device data.

⚙️

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To process Scan & Pay transactions between users and vendors
  • To fulfil mobile recharge and BBPS bill payment requests
  • To process vendor wallet settlements to their bank accounts
  • To track and credit referral rewards
  • To respond to support requests sent to our email
  • To comply with Indian legal and regulatory requirements
🔗

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party service provider to fulfil recharge and bill payment services:

  • IMWallet – Used for processing mobile prepaid recharge and BBPS (Bharat Bill Payment System) transactions. Relevant transaction data (mobile number, operator, amount) is shared with IMWallet solely to complete your requested service.

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to any other third parties. We do not use any third-party analytics, crash reporting, or advertising SDKs.

🔒

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These include:

  • Encrypted storage of passwords and sensitive credentials
  • Masked display of PAN, Aadhaar, and account numbers in the app interface
  • Secure HTTPS connections for all API communication
  • Access controls limiting data access to authorised personnel only

All data is stored on servers located within India.

🗓️

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Upon account deletion request, we will remove your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law (e.g., transaction records for tax/audit purposes).
