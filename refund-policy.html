Refund & Cancellation Policy

Overview

This policy describes the refund and cancellation terms for all services offered by Indian Utility Services. Please read this carefully before making any transactions.

Refund requests are handled exclusively via email and must be submitted within the applicable window stated below.

1. Account Activation Fee

Accessing the full features of Indian Utility Services requires a one-time account activation fee paid at the time of registration.

⚠️ Non-Refundable Once Activated: Once your account has been reviewed and activated by an administrator, the activation fee is strictly non-refundable under any circumstances.

✅ Pre-Activation Window: If your account has not yet been activated and you wish to cancel within 10 days of payment, you may request a refund by emailing us with your registered phone number and payment proof.

📷

2. Scan & Pay Transactions

Scan & Pay transactions are processed through our internal payment system. These are not UPI transactions and cannot be reversed through any external payment network.

Once a payment is confirmed:

  • The amount is immediately credited to the vendor's wallet
  • The transaction cannot be cancelled or recalled by the user
  • No automated refund mechanism exists for completed payments

Dispute Window: If you believe a Scan & Pay transaction was processed in error (e.g. wrong amount, duplicate payment), you must raise a dispute via email within 10 days of the transaction date. All disputes are reviewed manually and resolved at our discretion.

⚠️ We strongly recommend verifying the amount before confirming any Scan & Pay payment. Confirmed transactions cannot be reversed.

📱

3. Mobile Recharge

Mobile recharge services are processed via our third-party provider, IMWallet. Once a recharge is submitted and successfully processed, it cannot be cancelled or refunded.

Failed Recharges: If a recharge fails after the amount has been deducted from your wallet, the amount will be automatically credited back or you may raise a complaint within 24 hours of the transaction via email with your transaction details.

Refund timelines for failed recharges depend on IMWallet's processing time and may take up to 5–7 working days.

🧾

4. BBPS Bill Payments

Bill payments made via the Bharat Bill Payment System (BBPS) are processed through IMWallet. Once a bill payment is successfully submitted to the biller, it is final and non-reversible.

Failed Payments: If a bill payment fails and the amount is deducted, please contact us within 24 hours with your transaction reference. We will coordinate with IMWallet to resolve the issue.

📌 Always verify your biller details (account number, consumer number) before submitting a bill payment. Payments made to incorrect billing details cannot be recovered.

🏦

5. Vendor Settlements

Vendor wallet settlements are processed manually by our admin team and transferred to the vendor's registered bank account.

Once a settlement has been initiated and transferred to the bank account, it cannot be reversed through the platform. Any discrepancies must be reported within 10 days of the settlement date via email.

📬

6. How to Request a Refund

All refund and dispute requests must be submitted by email only. Please include the following in your email:

  • Your registered phone number
  • Transaction date and approximate amount
  • Nature of the issue (e.g. failed recharge, duplicate payment)
  • Any supporting screenshots or transaction references

📧 Send refund requests to:
support@indianutilityservices.in

We aim to respond within 2–3 working days. Approved refunds are processed within 7–10 working days.
